Hello everyone!

This is Warren House Maintenance Project. First reason I created this, is to make our work easier. Every department of this company has some technical issues; loose screws on firedoors, printer with jammed paper, hanging spot light on ceiling, restroom flush not working propely or outdoor issues. I had on my mind all of this. I created this application to make it easier to organise and fix your problems. Welcome to my Application!
There are four groups of issues:

Garden Maintenance Plumbing Electric

Before you add anything, decide to which group your problem belongs. For example:

  • You noticed one spot light in bedroom, lamp without light bulb, socket that is not working or anything connected to power - Electric.

  • You've seen that one of the table in terrace is wobbly, parasol has broken parts or any other issues from outdoors - Garden.

  • You've noticed that door knob is about to fall out, some screws are loose or any other actions with hammer or screwdriver - Maintenance.

  • You've just discovered that toilet is blocked, tap is dripping or there is slippery surface because of some leak - Plumbing.
